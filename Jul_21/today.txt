1. mix phx.gen.auth
What was done:
- Used mix phx.gen.auth to add authentication to the Phoenix app.

Purpose:
- To quickly integrate a pre-built authentication system with minimal setup.

Outcome:
- User login, registration, password hashing, and session management are now set up and ready to use.

Benefits:
- Saves development time.
- Provides secure password handling out of the box.
